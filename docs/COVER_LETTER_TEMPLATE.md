## üéØ WHEN TO USE A COVER LETTER

**Always include:**
- When application specifically requests one
- For jobs you're really excited about
- When applying directly to hiring manager (email)
- For companies where you have a referral

**Optional:**
- Quick-apply buttons (LinkedIn, Indeed)
- High-volume applications
- When you're tailoring resume specifically to job

**Never send:**
- Generic, un-customized cover letter
- Cover letter with wrong company name (embarrassing!)
- Cover letter longer than 1 page

---

## üí° PRO TIPS

1. **Research the company:**
   - Check their blog, Twitter, recent news
   - Find something specific to mention
   - Shows you did your homework

2. **Find the hiring manager:**
   - LinkedIn search: "[Company] [Department] manager"
   - Address by name, not "Dear Hiring Team"
   - Much more personal

3. **Match their language:**
   - Use terms from job description
   - Mirror their company voice (formal vs casual)
   - Shows you're a cultural fit

4. **Tell a micro-story:**
   - Not just "I built ShelfQuest"
   - "I built ShelfQuest in 90 days because [motivation]"
   - Stories are memorable

5. **End with action:**
   - "I'll follow up next week"
   - "I'm available Tuesday-Thursday for a call"
   - Shows initiative
